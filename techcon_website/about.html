    The term "TechCon" refers to several different organizations and events related to technology. One prominent example is TechCon North America, which has been held since 1996. Other variations include Radio TechCon, a conference focused on radio and audio technology, and Arm DevSummit, formerly known as DevCon or Arm TechCon. Additionally, there are companies like Tech-Con Systems, Inc. and Techcon, which also use "TechCon" in their names.

    Mission
